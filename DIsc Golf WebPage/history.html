About

I first played disc golf in 2016, or maybe it was 2018. I honestly can't remember. I started out with an Innova starter set; the leopard, the shark, and the aviar. I played two rounds with those and they sat in my basement for years.

In summer 2022, I picked up the discs again and hit a local course. I shot 14 over, but I had a blast. I started researching (and buying) more discs, and the hobby turned into an obsession. I currently bag 19 discs, and have many more on a shelf.
